Key Features

Why BLOOPER?

A dead-simple ripple effect that's built for live performance — trigger it on the beat, stack it for impact, and keep it locked to your music.

◉

Bump to Fire

One button sends a ripple radiating across your video. Map it to a MIDI pad or automation and fire it live, right on the beat.

⟐

Stack Up to 16

Fire ripples rapidly and up to 16 travel at once. Where they cross they reinforce — producing richer distortion for hits and drops.

◈

BPM & Bar Sync

Lock ripple speed and cadence to your track. BPM-synced and Bar-triggered modes keep your visuals perfectly in time.

⬡

Auto Loop

Turn on Auto Loop for hands-free, steady pulsing — no clicking required. Dial in the loop rate to match your vibe.

◇

True Circles, Any Canvas

Ripples stay perfectly round on 16:9, vertical, or square comps. No stretching, no egg-shapes — regardless of resolution.

⏣

Clip, Layer or Comp

Drop it anywhere in your effect chain. Shape the result with Speed, Ring Width, Amplitude, Center, and Decay controls.
